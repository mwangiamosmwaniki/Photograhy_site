Meet Our Team

Our Philosophy: The Art of Observation

Hello! We are the creative team behind Lens & Light. For over a decade, our passion has been capturing fleeting moments and transforming them into timeless works of art. We believe the best photographs aren't staged—they're observed. Authentic moments of connection, joy, and profound emotion guide everything we create.

Our journey began with a simple film camera passed down through family—an origin that sparked our dedication to this craft. Today, we specialize in a minimalist, cinematic style that embraces natural light and true-to-life color grading. Our goal is to create imagery that feels warm, intentional, and meaningful.

A Few Fun Facts

  • We shoot primarily with professional full-frame mirrorless systems.
  • Our favorite subject? Candid laughter during celebrations.
  • When we're not shooting, you’ll find us hiking and chasing the perfect landscape shot.
