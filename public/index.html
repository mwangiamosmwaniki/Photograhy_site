How We Work

Our streamlined process ensures a smooth, enjoyable experience from consultation to final delivery. We handle every detail so you can focus on enjoying your special moments.

1

Consultation

We start with a detailed conversation to understand your vision, preferences, and specific requirements. This helps us tailor our approach to match your unique style and expectations.

2

Planning

Together, we'll create a comprehensive shot list, timeline, and logistics plan. We scout locations if needed and ensure every detail is accounted for before your session or event.

3

Photo Session

On the day, we arrive early, work efficiently, and capture authentic moments. Our unobtrusive style ensures natural, candid shots while also creating beautiful posed portraits when needed.

4

Editing & Delivery

Your images are professionally edited with our signature style, color-corrected, and delivered through a private online gallery. Prints and albums are available upon request.
